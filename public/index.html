Bed-Stuy Strong

We are a mutual aid network of over 2,500 volunteers and neighbors from across Bed-Stuy who are supporting our community during the COVID-19 crisis.

Donate

One hundred percent of donations to Bed-Stuy Strong are used to purchase groceries and basic supplies for neighbors in need. These groceries and supplies are then dropped off by a network of hundreds of delivery volunteers – ordinary Bed Stuy residents who are looking to help members of their community.

We would be grateful for any contribution you can afford. We accept financial donations using any of these platforms:

Venmo: bedstuystrong (last 4 digits of phone number: 7668)
Cash App: $bedstuystrong
Google Pay: fund@bedstuystrong.com
Zelle: fund@bedstuystrong.com

Want to donate another way? Email us at fund@bedstuystrong.com.

Connect with us

If you’re interested in joining us, please fill out our volunteer sign up form and join us on Slack.

Our online Slack community is a place for neighbors to connect, share information, organize virtual events, and coordinate mutual aid efforts.

We are here to help – if you are unable to afford food or basic supplies, or if you are immunocompromised and cannot leave home, you can reach us via text or voicemail at (732) 771-9663 or by email at bedstuystrong@bedstuystrong.com and we will do our best to assist you.
